    ASEFA Overview
    ASEFA PUBLIC COMPANY LIMITED was established in 1997 as a leading manufacturer and provider of Electrical Power Distribution, Switchboard, Automation and Energy Efficient Solutions as well as After Sales and Integrated Engineering Services. The company's production facilities are located in Bangkok area. As of today, the company has approximately 1,200 employees.
    It is our commitment to respond to the expectation of our customers by providing them the best product with our growing and dynamic organization.
    Furthermore, according to our business plan, the company also searches for other good opportunities for related business growth.
    With our corporate philosophy, ASEFA efficiently runs our business by focusing on Product Development, Human Resources Development, Environment, and Social Responsibility to maintain the balance between economics return and social contribution.
